About US

A reflection to our purpose and personality of the business

  • 2019-2020

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Epic things start with small humble steps, and we never stopped dreaming of creating a world of travel and working remotely.

  • March 2020

    An Agency is Born

    We took the burden of having to figure out an itinerary  of you without breaking banks,  we started customizing our trip structure, destination and duration to suit the nomad squad.

  • December 2021

    Transition to Full Service

    We contracted one of the most brilliant Software Developer in Kenya, who we sat down and created one of the best platform for digital nomads and his brilliance paid off. Through awareness, member started contacting us to gather more information of our packages and slowly our digital caravan family was born.   

  • July 2022

    Phase Two Expansion

    The balancing act comes to play when new demands for growing business, to accommodate more diverse clientele and strengthening customer relationships. We are building a high quality team of people who have special skill sets who will establish more destination zone not just from Kenya but other regions beyond that offer incredible experience.
